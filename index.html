    Our Training

    Training

    We offer a comprehensive training program that covers all aspects of the game, including technical skills, tactical awareness, and physical fitness. Our training sessions are led by AIFF-approved coaches who have a wealth of experience in developing young footballers.

    Equipment

    We provide our players with high-quality training equipment, including balls, boots, and kits. This ensures that our players have the best possible equipment to train and play with.


    Match Experience

    We organize frequent match experiences for our players so that they can gain valuable experience and develop their skills. These match experiences can be against other academies, local clubs, or even professional teams.

    Motivation

    We offer motivational classes to our players to help them stay motivated and focused on their goals. We also provide them with support and guidance to help them overcome challenges.
